About BNY:

BNY (NYSE: BK) is a global financial services company that makes money work for the world. Managing it, moving it, and keeping it safe. For 240 years BNY put its expertise and platforms to work to help clients achieve their ambitions. Today BNY helps over 90% of Fortune 100 companies and the top 100 banks globally access the money they need. As of September 2024, BNY oversees $52.1 trillion in assets under custody and $2.1 trillion in assets under management.

ValueForm is excited to partner with BNY to elevate its trading systems and execution services. Together, we’re creating more seamless, efficient, and instinctive experiences for BNY’s clients and traders.
