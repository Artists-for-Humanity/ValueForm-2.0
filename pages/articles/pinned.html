About BNY:

BNY (NYSE: BK) is a global financial services company that makes money work for the world. Managing it, moving it, and keeping it safe. For 240 years BNY put its expertise and platforms to work to help clients achieve their ambitions. Today BNY helps over 90% of Fortune 100 companies and the top 100 banks globally access the money they need. As of September 2024, BNY oversees $52.1 trillion in assets under custody and $2.1 trillion in assets under management.

BNY taps ValueForm to better its trading systems and execution services. January 20th, 2025

ValueForm is excited to partner with BNY to elevate its trading systems and execution services. Together, we’re creating more seamless, efficient, and instinctive experiences for BNY’s clients and traders.

Markets are a game of seconds, where usability issues can cost traders time and ultimately millions of dollars. BNY’s decision to partner with us reflects its commitment to achieving business outcomes through human-centered thinking.

“Our experience designing complex systems, specifically in financial markets, makes this a great fit.” said Christopher Pross, our Managing Partner. “We’re excited to help BNY optimize its tools and drive efficiency and outcomes.”

Our experience designing complex systems, specifically in financial markets, makes this a great fit.
Christopher Pross Managing Partner, ValueForm

The partnership will focus on:

  • Systems Optimization: We’ll analyze and adjust existing back-end processes and infrastructure to create smoother, more intuitive user-facing experiences.
  • Creating and Testing New Experience Models: We’ll experiment with new and unconventional paradigms for traders engaging with markets. Going beyond functionality to make tools more instinctive, intuitive, and minimize mental effort.
  • User-Centered Roadmaps: We’ll create and sequence roadmaps based on user needs, ensuring alignment with trader and client expectations. These roadmaps will de-risk and guide the engineering effort by ensuring its alignment with users.

We’re thrilled about this collaboration and can’t wait to share updates as we shape the future of trading tools together.
